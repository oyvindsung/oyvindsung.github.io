## Schrödinger equation

$$
\begin{equation}
  i\hbar\frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial t}=\frac{\hbar^{2}\partial^{2}\Psi}{2m\partial x^{2}}+V\Psi
\end{equation}
$$

citation\cite{Knill E. Quantum computing[J]. Nature, 2010, 463(7280): 441-443.}
